About me

I'm currently a Ph.D. Candidate at Department of Information & Communication Engineering/電子情報学専攻, The University of Tokyo, supervised by Prof. Toyotaro Suzumura. Before that, I received my master degree under the supervision of Prof. Ryosuke Shibasaki and Prof. Renhe Jiang at Center for Spatial Information Science/空間情報科学研究センター, The University of Tokyo. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science in 2019 from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech, 南方科技大学), Shenzhen, China, where I was also a member of Database Group@SUSTech, supervised by Prof. Bo Tang.

Research Interests: All about Trajectory and Mobility Data.
