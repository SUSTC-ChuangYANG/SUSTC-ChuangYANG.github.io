About me

I'm currently an M1 Student at Center for Spatial Information Science, The University of Tokyo, supervised by Prof. Ryosuke Shibasaki and Prof. Renhe Jiang. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science in 2019 from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech, 南方科技大学), Shenzhen, China, where I was also a member of Database Group@SUSTech, supervised by Prof. Bo Tang.
My current research interests are in the area of Spatial-Temporal Data Visualization, Spatial-Temporal Data Mining. Especially interested in equipping interactive data exploration analytics tech to other domains (e.g., Disaster Prevention, Transportation, and Energy), with which domain experts could apply in-depth analysis and discover insights more efficiently.
Wish to collaborate with researchers from diverse disciplinary backgrounds, draw on interdisciplinary perspectives to break the disciplinary paradigm's limitations and solve the problem.
